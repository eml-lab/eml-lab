[Under Review]

Development of a novel conditional Markov chain framework to analyze intergenerational income mobility, providing new insights into the dynamics of economic inequality and social mobility.

Support Vector Machines for Large Scale Binary Choice Models

with Joon Y. Park, Yoosoon Chang

[Journal of Econometrics, 2024]

This paper introduces a novel approach to estimating large-scale binary choice models using support vector machines. We develop new theoretical results for the asymptotic properties of our estimator and demonstrate its superior performance in finite samples.
